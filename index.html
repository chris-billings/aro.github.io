how it works

From still image to
animated scene — in minutes

01

Upload your character

Any 2D character illustration. Webtoon, concept art, OC — if you drew it, Aro can animate it.

02

Generate multiple perspectives

Use atlas to generate different perspectives of your character, matching your exact style. Then simply adjust the front rig and Aro projects it to all other views—side, back, three quarter angles—automatically.

03

Animate & export

Pick a motion, preview your character moving in real time across all angles, then export a shareable animation clip.

features

Built for artists,
not technical users

✦

Auto-segmentation

Every limb, body part, and layer is automatically separated and cleaned. No masking, no Photoshop — just your original design.

⟳

2.5D perspective system

Aro generates front, three-quarter, and side views from a single illustration — your character moves through space convincingly.

◈

Auto-rigging

The rig is built automatically once you upload. No bones to place, no weights to paint. It just works.

↗

Video export

Export a clean, shareable animation clip. Post it directly to your social, portfolio, or webtoon — ready for an audience.

⬡

Motion library

Idle, walk, wave, jump and more — with new motions added regularly. Built for expressive 2D characters.

✧

Browser-native

Nothing to install. Aro runs entirely in your browser — on any device, any OS. Open the link and start animating.
